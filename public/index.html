Properties

Add all the properties you manage here. Each property can be assigned to one or more landlords who will receive booking notifications. Tap the + button to add your first property.

Landlords

Add the property owners you work with. Each landlord needs a name and email address for notifications. Their payout percentage determines how much of each booking's revenue goes to them.

Guests

Manage your guest profiles here. Track booking history, add notes, and contact guests directly via WhatsApp or email. Repeat guests are automatically highlighted.

Settings

Configure your profile, email notifications (Resend API), WhatsApp messaging, default check-in/out times, and currency. You can also export/import your data for backup.
